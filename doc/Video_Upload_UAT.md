## USERs

### Outline

1. Users will be asked to record a video of a one-rep form for a minimum of 5 select exercises chosen from in-scope exercises.
2. Users will then be presented with the application already logged in with a user who has had a fitness plan already created.
3. Users will be asked only to upload each video sequentially to get a score for their form. They are to only follow what the app presents, with minimal help from dev. Afterwards they will answer the following questions:
   - a. Did your videos upload successfully (y/n)?
   - b. After each video upload, were you returned to a page where you could see your score (y/n)?
   - c. After b., are you presented with an overall score out of 100 (y/n)?
   - d. After b. and c., are you additionally presented with insights on your form for target video (y/n)?
   - e. Do you feel the score is reflective of how you felt you completed target exercises (free response)?
   - f. Do you feel like insights given on what went well, what went wrong, and what to do next time, are relevant to you and helpful (free response)?
   - g. How easy was the process of tracing through the application to upload your video and get a score for your form? (Please answer with a number 1–5, 1 being the application was extremely difficult to follow, and 5 being the application was extremely easy to follow)
   - h. Do you have further comments on either the scoring outputs you received, how the application presented itself, or the ease of use of the application (free response)?
